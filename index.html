About

Artificial sweeteners are promoted as weight-loss aids, but some researchers suspect that artificial sweeteners actually make you gain weight. Animal research suggests that sweet tastes, whether from sugar or artificial sweeteners, tells your metabolism to get ready to digest carbohydrates. Hormones are secreted, but there's nothing for the hormones to do if real calories never show up.
